Roomba-GPT: Help Build the Underdog of AI Robotics

The Mission

We want to achieve a technology milestone: put ChatGPT into a Roomba! Our project will prove that it's possible to take an old Roomba vacuum from a thrift store and infuse it with ChatGPT's newly released AI models. We’re building a talking AI robot that moves, listens, and teaches — built entirely from recycled parts, Raspberry Pi, Arduino, and open-source software. It’s privacy-first, offline, and designed for education — proving you don’t need billion-dollar labs to innovate.

The Underdog Story

This isn’t a corporate lab project. It’s a small, independent build proving that innovation can come from the ground up. With your help, Roomba-GPT can become a platform for anyone curious about AI and robotics.

Thank You

Every donation — big or small — pushes this project forward and helps bring open, privacy-first robotics to life.
