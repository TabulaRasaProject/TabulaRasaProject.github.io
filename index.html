Privacy-First AI Robotics

Phase 1: Roomba-GPT

Our Mission

With your help, we can make history as the first team to put ChatGPT into a Roomba—turning a used vacuum into a private, voice-controlled AI assistant. Built from open-source code and recycled parts, it’s more than a robot: it’s proof that technology can be open, offline, and empowering.

Your Support Fuels

  • Sensors, mics, and better components
  • Build guides + tutorial videos
  • Hosting and ready-to-flash OS images
  • Classroom + maker-space test builds

Why It Matters

  • Proof-of-concept: AI at home can be safe, private, affordable
  • Ethical AI: entirely local, no cloud service — your data stays private
  • 100% open-source
  • Affordable, built from salvage
  • Ideal for students, makers, classrooms
